# Design System: BonBern (Framer-inspired)
**Project ID:** 9318632301390610859

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The atmosphere is **Elite, Immersive, and Deep**. It follows a **Premium Dark** philosophy, utilizing a pitch-black foundation with luminous white typography and high-energy red accents. The density is **Spacious but Impactful**, with large typography and bold geometric blocks. It feels like a high-end physical studio or a luxury cinematic experience.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Deep Obsidian (#000000):** The primary background color. Used to provide an infinite, focused canvas.
*   **Pure Stark White (#FFFFFF):** Primary text and icon color. Provides maximum contrast and a clean, high-end feel.
*   **Action Red (#E23028):** The brand's signature accent. Used for critical CTAs, focus points, and as a symbolic bridge between behavioral science and high-stakes performance.
*   **Muted Slate-Beige (#94A3B8 / #FFFCEC):** Used for secondary text or subtle borders and dividers to maintain hierarchy without clutter.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Headlines:** **Inter (Extra Bold/Black)**. Large scale, tight letter-spacing (-0.05em), and high impact.
*   **Body:** **Inter (Medium/Regular)**. Clean, highly readable, with generous line height (1.6) and structured alignment.
*   **Navigation:** All-caps or title-case with tracked-out letter-spacing for a sophisticated professional look.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Rectangular with **sharp or subtly rounded corners (8px)**. Primary buttons use Action Red (#E23028) with white text. Secondary buttons use transparent backgrounds with white borders (Ghost style).
*   **Cards (Bento Style):** Large, structured containers with **glassmorphism effects**. Backgrounds are deep translucent black (#000000 at 0.03 opacity) with thin, whisper-soft borders (#FFFFFF at 0.1 opacity).
*   **Imagery:** High-contrast, monochromatic or desaturated photography with red color-grading or accents.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Grid:** 12-column grid with generous gutters.
*   **Spacing:** Large vertical margins (8rem - 10rem) between sections to allow content "breathing room."
*   **Alignment:** Mixed alignment — centered for Hero/CTA, and asymmetric/left-aligned for detailed content blocks to create visual interest.
